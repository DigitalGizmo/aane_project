African Americans in the Record

Evidence from 18th Century Massachusetts

This database includes over 2,000 African American-related entries from the 18th century in Western Massachusetts. Emphasis is on Deerfield, Massachusetts where, by the mid-18th century, 38% of households included the enslaved. The earliest record dates to 1695. More..
